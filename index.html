The operating model

Not a course. A real-world problem solving studio.

Each batch forms teams, studies real contexts, ships weekly evidence, works with real customer problems, and receives AI-assisted individual coaching.

01

Selective admissions

Students are filtered for curiosity, coachability, communication potential, and willingness to build daily.

02

Team ventures

Thirty students become six cross-functional teams, each with clear roles and weekly measurable output.

03

Individual attention

Every student gets a baseline profile, weekly check-in, role scorecard, portfolio, and skill-gap feedback.

04

AI monitored

Daily logs, artifacts, commits, interviews, and demo quality feed AI reports that help mentors intervene early.
